BrowserPlus

Inventing the future of browsing

Break Free from Browser History

When you use BrowserPlus, the only thing that appears in your history is an entry named "BrowserPlus." Nothing else appears, giving you full freedom to browse without worrying about your friends peeking at your history.

Discourage "Screen Peekers"

With a simple keystroke, it is easy to blur the contents of your browser. If someone begins to peek over your shoulder, simply press Alt + B to hide what you are doing. The only way to undo this action is to use the shortcut again or click a button onscreen.

Hover over the image to the left to see what the Privacy Blur is hiding.

Nightime Browsing, Made Simple

With the click of a button, BrowserPlus dims to complete darkness. Feel free to browse as late as you want, without straining your eyes or waking others up.

Close Without Pesky Popups

Don't want someone to know what about your browsing? With the click of the button, BrowserPlus instantly closes. If it cannot close, it navigates to a blank page.
